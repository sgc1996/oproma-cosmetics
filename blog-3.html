Entrepreneurial Excellence Awards 2024

Oproma Cosmetics Honored at Entrepreneurial Excellence Awards 2024

We are thrilled to announce that Oproma received two prestigious awards at the Entrepreneurial Excellence Awards 2024, a celebration of innovation and leadership in the business world. These awards reflect our commitment to excellence in luxury body care and recognize the visionary leadership behind our success.

Best Luxury Body Care Brand in Sri Lanka (Oplee Luxury Products) – 2023/2024

Our Oplee Luxury Products line was honored with the title of Best Luxury Body Care Brand in Sri Lanka for 2023/2024. This recognition is a testament to our dedication to crafting high-quality, luxurious body care products that set a new standard in skincare. Our Oplee products, made with premium ingredients and advanced formulations, continue to provide an unparalleled skincare experience to our customers across Sri Lanka.

Certified Entrepreneur (Honorary) – Mr. Pradeep Mapalagama

Our Managing Director, Mr. Pradeep Mapalagama, received the Certified Entrepreneur (Honorary) award, acknowledging his exceptional leadership and entrepreneurial vision throughout 20 years. This award highlights Mr. Mapalagama’s contributions to the company’s growth and his role in positioning Oproma as an industry leader in luxury body care.

Continuing the Journey of Excellence

These honors inspire us to strive for even greater achievements as we continue delivering exceptional products and pushing boundaries in the industry. We extend our gratitude to everyone who has been a part of our journey, from our loyal customers to our dedicated team and partners.

This year's event was again a huge success.
