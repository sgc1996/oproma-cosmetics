Donation for Needy Families During Covid 19

Phase 01

Beneficiaries : Families around Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte

Date of Commencement : 27.08.2020

Date of Completion : 27.08.2020

We donated We donated as many dry rations as we could to the needy people around the city of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte during the disastrous COVID-19 period. We hope to continue these tasks in the future as well, until this situation subsides.

Phase 02

Beneficiaries : Families around Kirikiththa Weliweriya

Date of Commencement :01.07.2021

Date of Completion : 05.07.2021

We donated as many dry rations as we could to the needy people around the city of Weliweriya, joining the staff at Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd. All the staff members gathered to pack the rations, and the goods were delivered around the village. Our sole aim was to make sure that none of the families around our factory area were suffering from hunger during these hard days of COVID-19.

අපි Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd හි කාර්ය මණ්ඩලය සමඟ එකතු වෙමින් වැලිවේරිය නගරය අවට අසරණ මිනිසුන්ට හැකි පමණින් වියළි සලාක පරිත්‍යාග කළෙමු. සියලුම කාර්ය මණ්ඩල සාමාජිකයින් සලාක ඇසුරුම් කිරීමට රැස් වූ අතර භාණ්ඩ ගම වටා බෙදා හරින ලදී. අපගේ ඒකායන අරමුණ වූයේ කොවිඩ්-19 හි මෙම දුෂ්කර දිනවලදී අපගේ කර්මාන්තශාලා ප්‍රදේශය අවට කිසිදු පවුලක් කුසගින්නෙන් පෙළෙන්නේ නැති බවට වග බලා ගැනීමයි.
