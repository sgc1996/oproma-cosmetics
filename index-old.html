OPROMA OUR STORY


We, Oproma Cosmetics(Pvt)Ltd, is a GMP, ISO (9001-2015), and SLS 1672:2020 certified skincare, haircare and healthcare products manufacturing firm in Sri Lanka that distributes locally and exports internationally since 2012. We provide contract manufacturing services to a variety of well-known Sri Lankan firms.
